# Top Level Scopes and what they are for

## `.id` - People

Entities like VeroSelf create personal registries here

## `.asset` - Things

For tracking physical things in the real world, or digital assets. eMotor.ID uses `emotor.asset` scope for it's records.

## `.org` - Organizations

Trusted institutionalization. Think Pepsi or Harvard

## `.event` - Record of Events

This is for pure logging of something occuring. Most things that don't fit elsewhere go here.

## `.gov` - Governing Agencies

Most 2 digit country codes accepted with some sort of contact to HodeauxLedger staff.

## `.chain` - Chain of Trust

`core.chain` is the core trust.
`root.chain` is the root authorities.

## `.token` - Value based Assets

## `.document` - Documents
