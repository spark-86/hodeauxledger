**Temporal Cryptophysics** is a new field of study and engineering that combines **cryptographic certainty** with the **irreversibility of time**, establishing a unified system for recording truth across time, space, and consensus.

## 🌌 What Is It?

**Temporal Cryptophysics** is the study and application of immutable, cryptographically-linked data structures bound by a linear notion of time — not just as a timestamp, but as a foundational ordering principle of the universe.

It is the intersection of:

- **Cryptography**: Using public-key signatures to prove authorship, ownership, and non-repudiation of data.
- **Physics of Time**: Recognizing the unidirectional, causal nature of time as a structuring force.
- **Information Theory**: Treating data as energy that must be preserved, transformed, and reasoned about through state changes.

Temporal Cryptophysics does not merely log data.  
It **crystallizes** it — encoding human, machine, and institutional actions into a permanent, inspectable lattice.

## 🔐 The Time-Stamped Truth Machine

Each record (called a `R⬢`) is:

- **Append-only**: Time flows forward; so does the ledger.
- **Signed**: Each entry is cryptographically sealed by its originator(s).
- **Chained**: Every record references the hash of its predecessor.
- **Scoped**: Reality is broken into namespaces, identities, and authorities.
- **Auditable**: Every fact, every change, every choice is retained forever.

In this world, **there is no rewrite** — only append.
